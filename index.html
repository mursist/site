          Frequently Asked Questions

          Everything You Need to Know About Our Services

          What is the difference between Taxi Service and Executive Car Service?

          Taxi Service is an older and smaller vehicle with taxi signages on the vehicle and decals. Casual dressed driver.

          Executive Car Service is a newer Cadillac Escalade, plain black, no signs or decals on the vehicle. Uniformed dressed driver. Water, newspaper and WiFi provided in the vehicle.

          Where do you meet at the airport?

          We meet inside the baggage claim area with a sign with passenger's last name.

          Do you monitor the flights on the arival if there are any delays or cancellations?

          Yes, we do. We will arrive at the airport when your delayed flight will land.
